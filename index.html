Mint Chocolate Brownies Recipe

Rich, Fudgy, and Minty — Perfect for chocolate lovers!

Makes 16 brownies

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Cook Time: 25-30 minutes

Total Time: 3.5/4 hours (includes cooling time)

Ingredients:

For the Brownies:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

For the Mint Fondant:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • A few drops of green food coloring (optional)

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Brownies: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch square baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter, then stir in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Add the flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Mix until smooth.
  3. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let the brownies cool.
  4. Prepare the Mint Fondant: Beat softened butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar, heavy cream, and peppermint extract. Spread over cooled brownies. Refrigerate.
  5. Prepare the Ganache: Heat chocolate chips and heavy cream until smooth. Spread over the mint layer and refrigerate until set.
  6. Serve: Once chilled, cut the brownies into squares and enjoy!
